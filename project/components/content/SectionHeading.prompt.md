**SectionHeading** — the recurring section opener: uppercase blue eyebrow, squared heading, optional intro paragraph.

```jsx
<SectionHeading
  eyebrow="Join a Team (K-12)"
  title="Explore and be inspired through STEAM"
  intro="Our teams are made up of students from grades K-12, and compete as part of FIRST®."
/>
```

Props: `eyebrow`, `title`, `intro`, `align` (`left`/`center`), `onDark`.
