**Input** — squared text field for email signup, contact and donation forms. Border turns Team Blue on focus.

```jsx
<Input label="Email" id="email" type="email" placeholder="you@example.com" hint="Join our email list" />
<Input label="Name" id="name" error="This field is required" />
```

Props: `label`, `hint`, `error`, `size` (`sm`/`md`/`lg`), plus all native input attributes.
